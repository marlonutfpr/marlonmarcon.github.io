About

Professor at the Federal University of Technology - Paraná (UTFPR) - Dois Vizinhos Campus.
Has experience in Computer Science with an emphasis on Computer Vision, Natural Language Processing (NLP), Machine Learning, Pattern Recognition and Applications. Main research areas include Machine Learning, Deep Learning, 2D and 3D Computer Vision.
